About OLI
Open Learning Intitiative

Open Learning Initiative (OLI) is created and maintained by the students of NSS, IITB.
As a part of OLI, we record videos in regional languages, explaining the concepts that school students would be taught by a teacher. Presently our YouTube channel has 170+ videos in 8 languages and 1500+ subscribers.
This site will serve as platform to download these videos and watch offline. Check the video to know how to use this site.
